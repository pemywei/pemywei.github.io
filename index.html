Biography

I am a Ph.D candidate at Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My research interests include Machine Translation, Natural Language Generation and Deep Learning. Currently, I focus on semantic-augmentation and learning deep models for NMT as well as learning universal representations across languages.
